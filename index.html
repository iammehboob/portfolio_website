nearserve — book a trusted plumber, electrician, babysitter or cleaner in minutes

nearserve is an on-demand local services marketplace built end-to-end in Flutter with a clean, layered architecture (lib/core/api). Customers browse verified providers by category, get instant notifications, and pay securely in-app — while a resilient API layer keeps the experience smooth even on flaky mobile networks.
